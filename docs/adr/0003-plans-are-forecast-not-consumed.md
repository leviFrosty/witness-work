# Plans are forecast; they are never consumed by Time Entries

**Day Plans** and **Recurring Plan** instances are pure records of intent. **Time Entries** are independent records of what actually happened. The two are never joined: a Plan is not "completed" or "consumed" when a Time Entry lands on its date, and a Time Entry has no concept of fulfilling a Plan. The app reconciles them visually (e.g., "you planned 10 hrs this week, you logged 7"). We considered linking Time Entries to Plans so the User could mark items done planner-style, and rejected it because real field service often diverges from the plan in time, duration, and segmentation — forcing a join would create hard-to-resolve mismatches and pressure the User to either edit the plan or skip logging when reality didn't match. Keeping the two streams independent lets the User log freely and lets the UI present the gap as information, not as an error.
